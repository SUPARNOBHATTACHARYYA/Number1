I am from West Bengal, India. I am a Ph.D. student at Pennsylvania State University in the Engineering Science and Mechanics Department (2014-present). I did my Masters from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur and my undergrad from Jadavpur University, Kolkata.<\/p>\r\n

My primary research interest is reduced-order modeling of dynamical systems. Currently, I am studying model order reduction of structural systems that are subjected to discontinuity and non-smooth loading conditions. Previously, I have worked on experimental determination of structural damping in metals. Additionally, I am interested in solid mechanics, finite element analysis, data-driven modeling, and nonlinear dynamics.<\/p>","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
